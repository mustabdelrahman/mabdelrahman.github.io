Programmable liquid crystal elastomers (LCEs)

Designing acrylate‑based mesogen mixtures with amine‑mediated chain extension to lower TNI and enable large, reversible actuation. Topics: oligomer/solvent co‑crosslinking, anisotropic printing, and thermal/mechanical programing.

  • Mix design: 10‑mesogen library with tunable nematic transitions
  • Direct ink writing with heated syringe (battery‑powered adapter)
  • Characterization: strain–temperature curves, fatigue, relaxation

Architected lattices for soft manipulation

Bilayer unit cells and rectilinear lattices that close upon heating and reopen on cooling for pick‑and‑place of LEDs and small components.

  • Design of 10‑cell grippers; open/close via thermal field
  • Sequential animations for debugging bilayer kinematics
  • Mechanical computing with materials‑level logic

Dual‑material printing with rotating nozzles

Custom nozzle with semi‑circular channels enabling in‑nozzle mixing and fiber orientation control for anisotropic response.

  • Alicat pressure control and Nordson high‑pressure adapter
  • UV‑crosslinkable middle layers and non‑bonding outer layers
  • On‑the‑fly alignment and filament architecting
