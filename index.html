Hello — I'm Mustafa.

I am a postdoctoral researcher working at the intersection of soft robotics, active materials, and liquid crystal elastomers (LCEs). My group will explore programmable matter that senses, computes, and actuates using intrinsic material physics.

I’m entering the faculty job market next cycle. I’m excited to collaborate on shape‑morphing polymers, architected lattices, and soft machines.

Research vision

We design autonomous soft machines by uniting synthesis of shape‑morphing polymers, architected materials for logic, and soft mechanical computing. Current work includes dual‑material 3D printing with rotating nozzles, bilayer lattices for pick‑and‑place manipulation, and LCE formulations with tunable nematic transitions.
